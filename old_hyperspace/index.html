Global Warming

Global Warming is becoming a more and more grave issue as the wold continues to industrialize.

Average temperatures have climbed 1.4 degrees Fahrenheit (0.8 degree Celsius) around the world since 1880, much of this in recent decades, according to NASA's Goddard Institute for Space Studies.

Social Movements

Many agree that social movements are essential to influence industrial and infrastructure changes, but noone actually wants to do anything.

How can we fix that?
It's simple. Increase local and global awareness. The more people that know about Global Warming, the more people will be willing to devote their time to creating social movements. The goal of this website is simple. Collect data about people's opinions on Global Warming, and inform them about how they can help and contribute to the movement.

Action Research

Action Research is a full year program that all 10th grade American Literature students in Millburn High School must participate in. Students pick out a social issue at the beginning of the year, and research it throughout the course of the year. This work culminates in an "action", each student somehow contributes to a solution to their social issue.
